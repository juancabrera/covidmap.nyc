How it works ?

Get notifications based on your zipcode

If you allow location access to the app you will receive notifications as you move from zipcode to zipcode. No data of your location will be shared or saved, it will be used only to give you the most relevant data.
If you dont want the app to access your location, that's fine, you can still explore the map.

Explore the map

Use the map to explore the latest official COVID-19 data by zipcode. Tap on it and access to the total number of tests preformed, the current active positives cases and the total deaths until today.
Data is updated daily with the official COVID data published by NYC.

About

NYC COVID map was designed and developed 100% by two individuals as a personal side project, there are no affiliations to any private or public organizations.

Privacy is protected by not saving or sending your location data. Location data is only used to detect if you move from one zip code to another and notify you with the latest COVID data of your current zone. You can still use the app without granting location access permissions.
